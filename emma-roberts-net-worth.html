Early Life and Family Background

Emma Rose Roberts was born into Hollywood royalty. Her father, Eric Roberts, and aunt, Julia Roberts, are both acclaimed actors. Growing up in this environment, it’s no wonder Emma found herself gravitating towards the spotlight.

Emma Roberts’ Acting Career

Emma’s journey in acting began at the tender age of nine with her debut in the film Blow (2001), where she played Johnny Depp’s daughter. This was just the beginning.

She rose to fame with her role as Addie Singer on Nickelodeon’s Unfabulous (2004-2007). This role not only made her a household name among teens but also opened doors to numerous opportunities in film and television.

Over the years, Emma has built a diverse portfolio. She starred in popular films such as Aquamarine (2006), Nancy Drew (2007), We’re the Millers (2013), and Scream 4 (2011).
