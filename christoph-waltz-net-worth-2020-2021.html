Early Life & Biography

Christoph Waltz was born in Vienna, Austria, on 4 October 1956, to his parents Johannes Waltz and Elisabeth Urbancic. His father was a German set designer while his mother was an Austrian costume designer. He comes from a family of artists and actors, including his maternal grandmother, who was a silent film actress, his great-grandfather, Georg Reimers, who was an actor.

When Waltz was seven years old, his father died, and later his mother got married to composer and conductor Alexander Steinbrecher. He was interested in music and theatre from a very young age and visited the opera twice a week.

Waltz completed his graduation from Vienna’s Theresianum, and then he went to the renowned Max Reinhardt Seminar to learn acting. At the same time, he took singing lessons from the University of Music and Performing Arts Vienna.
