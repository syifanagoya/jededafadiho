Biography

Robert Rihmeek Williams (born May 6, 1987), addressed as Meek Mill, is known for his profession as an American rapper and songwriter, but he is not just limited to his musical talent. He has also ventured into entrepreneurship and activism, showcasing his multifaceted abilities. 

Throughout his career, he has released an impressive collection of seven studio albums, each resonating with audiences and achieving commercial success.

Furthermore, the industry has recognized Meek Mill‘s indisputable skill, as he has received significant prizes, such as the BET Hip Hop Award and a Grammy Award nomination.

Early Life & Education

Meek Mill, a highly skilled rapper known for his phenomenal talent, was born on May 6, 1987, in the vibrant neighbourhood of South Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. 

Unfortunately, his childhood was marked by hardships and obstacles. Tragically, Meek Mill lost his father, Robert Parker, at age five, leaving his mother, Kathy Williams, to singlehandedly raise him and his sister, Nasheema Williams. 

Despite the adversities he faced early in life, Meek Mill displayed tenacity and determination, which served as fundamental driving forces behind his rise to success. 

Regrettably, due to personal circumstances and challenges he encountered during his formative years, Meek Mill decided to drop out of Strawberry Mansion High School in Philadelphia after his sophomore year.

Career

Dreams and Nightmares, Meek Mill‘s debut album, debuted at the top of the Billboard 200 chart in 2012 and was a commercial success. It was given platinum certification by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA). 

His second album, Dreams Worth More Than Money, was released in 2015 and achieved commercial success by reaching number two on the Billboard 200 chart. It received double platinum certification from the RIAA.

Meek Mill released more music since his debut album, Dreams and Nightmares, in 2012, dropping four additional albums. These albums include Wins & Losses in 2017, Championships in 2018, Expensive Pain in 2020, and an as-yet-untitled album set to be released in 2021. 

Throughout his career, Mill has also worked alongside various notable artists such as Drake, Nicki Minaj, and Jay-Z, showcasing his versatility and collaborative spirit.

In addition to his successful music career, Meek Mill has unfortunately faced numerous legal challenges. He has encountered several arrests and subsequent imprisonments, mainly due to charges related to gun possession and probation violations. 

In 2018, he was freed from prison after serving a five-month sentence for violating his parole, which was a remarkable incident. His legal problems have indeed touched Meek Mill‘s personal and business life.

Personal Life

The 37-year-old Meek Mill has had a couple of high-profile partnerships throughout his career. From early 2015 to early 2017, he was in a relationship with Nicki Minaj. The pair was well-known for their public feuds, and their split was said to be painful. 

Meek Mill‘s romantic history includes relationships with Bernice Burgos in 2018, Milan Rouge Harris from 2017 to 2020, K. Michelle, Jai Nice from 2013 to 2014, Lira Mercer in 2013, Nicki Minaj from 2012 to 2016, and Fahimah Raheem from 2006 to 2012.

In addition, Meek Mill has had casual encounters with Briona Mae in 2014 and Trina. There have also been rumours of a connection between Meek Mill and Iesha Marie in 2014. 

Murad Williams and Rihmeek Williams are Meek Mill‘s two children. His son, Murad Williams‘ mother is Fahimah Raheem.
