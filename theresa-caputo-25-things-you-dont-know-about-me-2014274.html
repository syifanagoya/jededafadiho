Theresa Caputo: 25 Things You Don't Know About Me

Publish date: 2024-08-19

On TLC's Long Island Medium (Sundays, 9 P.M.), Theresa Caputo talks to spirits of the dead, all while keeping her hair perfectly teased. Here, the 46-year-old spiritual star reveals 25 things you don't know about her to Us Weekly. 

1. Yes, I speak to dead people. But I still have to sleep with a night-light. 

2. I recently conquered my fear of elevators.

3. You'll find me in church every Sunday. 

4. I really love my Vitamix. 

5. Treadmill, weight lifting, sit-ups, burpees: I work out four to five times a week. 

6. My favorite movie is Pretty Woman. 

7. I drink a minimum of six cups of coffee a day. 

8. I live next door to my parents on the same block where I grew up. 

9. I still use aerosol hair spray, but don't worry: I recycle the cans. 

10. My favorite NYC restaurant is Scarpetta. 

11. I love Broadway shows.

12. My favorite food is a good cheeseburger, served with no bun and no fries. 

13. I travel the country doing live shows.

14. The only book I ever read in its entirety was my own. I had no choice: I wrote it. 

15. I own 31 pairs of Christian Louboutins. 

16. Pinkberry is my new obsession. 

17. As a child, I played travel soccer. 

18. I get my nails manicured to be exactly 1 inch from cuticle to tip. 

19. BN (before nails), I was a guitarist in a folk group. 

20. I'm a Gemini. 

21. I first sensed spirits when I was 4 years old. 

22. I own Swarovski crystal-covered cowboy boots.
