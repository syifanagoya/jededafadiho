What is Mark Scheinberg's Net Worth?

Mark Scheinberg is an Israeli-Canadian businessman and entrepreneur who has a net worth of $4.1 billion. Mark Scheinberg was born in Israel in 1973. He is best known for being the co-founder and co-owner of the online gambling company PokerStars. Scheinberg moved to Toronto, Canada at 13 and entered the online gambling industry at 28.

The PokerStars parent company Rational Group Ltd. was bought by Amaya Gaming Group of Canada in 2014 for almost $5 billion. Scheinberg was 75% owner and CEO of Rational which also owned Full Tilt Poker but left the company due to the sale. He resides in the Isle of Man, United Kingdom.
