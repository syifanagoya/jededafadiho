Melcom branches in Accra

Publish date: 2024-02-05

Melcom is a supermarket chain consisting of 42 shops spread all over Ghana. It was started in 1989 by Indian magnate Bhagwan Khubchandani. His late father, Ramchand Khubchandani, had arrived in the then Gold Coast in 1929 as a 14-year-old to work as a store boy. Melcom Group of Companies is a family business.

The Melcom Group of Companies consists of six separate entities: Melcom Limited, Century Industries Limited, Crownstar Electronic Industries Limited, Melcom Hospitality, Melcom Travels, and Melcom Care. Aside from conquering an extensive retail market share with a network of 42 retail outlets spread all over Ghana (Melcom Limited), the Group is well-diversified in other businesses.

Melcom Group is best known for its shopping mall, Melcom Limited. Over the years, Ghana’s largest chain of retail department stores, Melcom offers thousands of products and hundreds of well-known brands such as Mobile phones and Tablets, Computers and Accessories, Television and Audio Appliances, Refrigerators and Freezers, Washing Machines, Air conditioners, Home and Kitchen Appliances, Lighting and Hardware, Furniture, Home and Kitchen Essentials, Home Decor and Furnishing, Luggage, Supermarket, Beverages, Household and Cleaning products, Beauty and personal care products.
